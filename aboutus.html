Our Vision

  • To make Heaven
  • To take as many people as possible with us
  • To have a member of RCCG in every family of all nations
  • To accomplish No.1 above, holiness will be our lifestyle

Our Mission

  • To plant churches within five minutes walking distance in every city and town of developing countries
  • To plant churches within five minutes driving distance in every city and town of developed countries
  • To pursue these objectives until every nation in the world is reached for Jesus Christ our Lord
